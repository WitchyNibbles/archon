# Project Idea Template

## Request Summary

What was the original customer or CEO request?

## Goal

What must be true when this work succeeds?

## Audience

Who is affected first?

## User segments

Who are the primary users or operators?

## Main pain

What painful problem does it solve?

## Core flows

What are the top 3 to 5 flows?

## Product acceptance checks

What user-visible checks would prove the outcome feels complete and polished on first try?

## Measurable success criteria

What observable checks would prove the work is successful?

## Constraints

Examples:

- compliance
- auth model
- target platform
- budget
- deadline
- integrations

## Non-goals

What is explicitly out of scope?

## Risks and unknowns

What is risky, ambiguous, or blocked?

## Trust boundaries

What data, roles, secrets, external systems, or destructive actions matter here?

## Stop/go

Is this safe to plan or implement right now?
